## Moderation Guidelines

### Community Culture We Want
- **Helpful:** Answer questions, share knowledge
- **Humble:** We're learning together
- **Honest:** Share failures, not just wins
- **Inclusive:** All skill levels welcome
- **Technical:** Depth over hype

### Response Time Goals
- **Critical bugs:** Within 1 hour
- **Support questions:** Within 4 hours (business hours)
- **Feature requests:** Within 24 hours
- **General chat:** When possible (no SLA)

### How to Handle Issues

**Negative/Toxic Members:**
1. DM privately: "Hey, noticed [behavior]. We're keeping discussions constructive."
2. Set clear expectations
3. If continues â†’ temporary mute (24h)
4. If persists â†’ ban

**Spam/Promotional Posts:**
1. Delete immediately
2. DM: "We don't allow promotional posts. Share in context only."
3. If repeats â†’ ban

**Off-Topic Discussions:**
1. Gentle redirect: "This is interesting! Let's move to #general"
2. Keep #help focused on support

**Demanding/Entitled Behavior:**
1. Set boundaries politely: "We're a small team doing our best!"
2. Explain prioritization process
3. Don't over-promise

### Engagement Best Practices

**DO:**
- Respond to every question (even if just "looking into this!")
- Share work-in-progress and behind-the-scenes
- Celebrate member contributions publicly
- Ask for feedback regularly
- Be transparent about challenges
- Use emojis (makes it feel friendly)
- Voice chat occasionally (builds connection)

**DON'T:**
- Leave questions unanswered for days
- Only show up to announce features
- Ignore negative feedback
- Over-promise features
- Argue with critics
- Use corporate/marketing speak
