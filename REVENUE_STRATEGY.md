## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Product-Market Fit
**Risk**: Developers don't want/need visual multi-agent orchestration
**Mitigation**: Validate with user interviews, iterate quickly, pivot if needed
**Fallback**: Position as internal tooling for companies (more specific use case)

### Risk 2: Technical Scalability
**Risk**: Platform can't handle multi-tenant load
**Mitigation**: Load testing, gradual rollout, resource limits per user
**Fallback**: Start with self-hosted model (lower operational burden)

### Risk 3: Competition
**Risk**: Existing players (LangGraph, CrewAI) add visual interfaces
**Mitigation**: Move fast, build community, differentiate on UX
**Fallback**: Partner with them (provide UI layer for their tools)

### Risk 4: Burn Rate
**Risk**: Revenue ramp too slow, run out of money
**Mitigation**: Aggressive customer acquisition, cut unnecessary costs
**Fallback**: Raise small angel round ($50K-$100K) to extend runway

### Risk 5: Team Capacity
**Risk**: Team overwhelmed by sales + support + development
**Mitigation**: Automate onboarding, comprehensive docs, community support
**Fallback**: Hire contractor for support/docs (if we have revenue)
