## Backup Plan (If No Hunter by Day 3)

### Option 1: Self-Launch (Acceptable)
- You can launch without a hunter
- Your account becomes the "maker"
- Actually preferred by some founders (more authentic)
- Can still be very successful

**Advantages:**
- Full control over timing
- Authentic founder story
- No coordination needed

**Disadvantages:**
- Less initial visibility (no hunter network)
- Have to build credibility yourself

### Option 2: Continue Hunter Search
- Expand to Tier 3 (up-and-coming hunters)
- Look for hunters with 1k-5k followers
- May have more availability
- Still valuable for network

### Option 3: Delay Launch
- If really want a specific hunter
- Push PH launch by 1 week
- Use extra time for polish
- Risk: Losing momentum from Show HN

**Recommendation:** Self-launch is fine! Many successful products launch without hunters. Focus on having a great product and engaging in comments.
